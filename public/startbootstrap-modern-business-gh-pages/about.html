About Foster Finder

How We Started

We came together through our love of animals, especially our dogs. Through discovery we found each of us had rescued our furbabies, giving them the second chance at life.

Foster Finder is currently based in the North Texas, Dallas, area as an avenue for those interested in fostering to search for foster dogs and more importantly for rescue groups and shelters to search for potential fosters.
